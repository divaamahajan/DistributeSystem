Innovating with a Mission: The Campaign for Santa Clara University

We invite you to support our mission and inspire the next generation of leaders through our Campaign for SCU. Any gift of any size counts towards the Campaign. Support what matters to you, whether it be scholarships and access, faculty investments, or student programs. 

Learn more about the Innovating with a Mission campaign. 

Support Scholarships

Making a premium Santa Clara University education affordable is an important aspect of the Jesuit faith tradition, and is paramount to expanding the University’s reach and boosting our national reputation. Together, with the help of our donors supporting scholarship funds, we can help high-achieving students from all backgrounds return and continue to earn their degree regardless of their or their family’s ability to pay.

Support Student Mental Health Programs

Implementing a thoughtful, strategic and forward-thinking student mental health and counseling program is our best tool for responding to the changing needs of our students.

Our systematic and interconnected approach to supporting student mental health includes programs to develop life skills; promoting social connectedness; identifying and responding to students at risk; increasing help-seeking behaviors and providing a comprehensive array of mental health and substance abuse resources.

Additional areas of program expansion: 

  • Addressing basic needs -- While we continue to support the Bronco Pantry and students who are food and housing insecure, our campus data suggests that an increasing number of students report food and housing insecurity.  This dimension of student mental health needs additional attention.  
  • Expanding peer-related mental health program -- The Wellness Center and Cowell both support peer education programs; however the growing interest among students suggests that we have unmet opportunity in this area

Support Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion at SCU

The entire Santa Clara family extends our heart-felt gratitude to the many Broncos among us who, in years past, have labored in their own ways to help create a truly equitable, inclusive and welcoming community. Step by step, your legacy is foundational to our transformation.

Today, as we honor and celebrate our diversity, teams of faculty, staff and students across the entire campus engage in projects guided by our newly-formed Division of Inclusive Excellence.  This important work is also reflected every day by the many campus organizations that support, cultivate and harness the diverse experiences of our students.
